About

I have a minor's degree in Arts, and in the process of getting my full Game Designer degree. I'm currently in Santiago, Chile and my biggest focus at the moment is writing stories, especially those that focus on my home country, Venezuela. You can see some of my work in the "Work" tab.

Work


Manabu

Manabu was my first experience with Unity. I had a lot of help with this project and was ultimately what made me realize what I wanted to pursue. It's a quiz game that was designed for middle schoolers to reinforce three specific subjects: English, Math, and Universal History. Ultimately, it was a success in its objective and I was pretty proud of it.

Mini Projects

I have a couple of mini-projects that I used to learn about new things. Some notable projects are: Pasillo Terror "Hallway of Terror", which focused on learning lights and smoke simulations; Nostalgia, was used to learn Cinemachine and Post Processing, and Domino was used to understand Unity Physics and enhance my Cincemachine skills.

Forgotten Royalty

Forgotten Royalty is a great assignment that taught me level construction and planning, it's a personalized Monument Valley level with some added lore and characters.

Ninja Heaven

Ninja Heaven is a small and quick project that taught me the basics of terrain, AI combat, and using blender to make some of the assets.

Life in Spads

Life in Spads, this project taught me about pre-production for games. I wrote a small Game Design Document and made a couple of rough sketches of the vision I had for a full game.

The Friendly Ghost

I developed The Friendly Ghost to create an innovative mechanic for a mobile game. You are a ghost in a house that was once your own, and your main objective is to scare away the burglars that have made their way inside.

El Batallón de Chacabuco

Play Demo

El Batallón de Chacabuco, "The Chacabuco battalion" is a graphic novel built on Twine that relives a historic moment in Chile's past. It's a telling of one of the greatest reminders of courage and coordination in the nation's battle against the Peruvian troops. With only 77 soldiers, the Chilean troops managed to hold their position for more than two days against three hundred enemy soldiers and a whole village of angry Peruvians.

Un viaje por la frontera

Play Demo

Un Viaje por la Frontera or "A Journey across the border" is one of the projects that I am most proud of. As a Venezuelan, I understand that not a lot of foreigners understand the real situation that has been transpiring in my country. This game tries to show the player through tough decisions and real-life based stories, the reality of the Venezuelan population that leaves their country to find a better life elsewhere.

Gloria al bravo pueblo

Demo

Gloria al bravo pueblo, "Glory to the brave people" is another project that tries to show the Venezuelan situation. You're a journalist that has recently been investigating the current situation of the Venezuelans. To obtain real-life experiences and in-depth information, you decide to interview Venezuelans and learn directly from the source. One of the Venezuelans will teach you some early history from the 70s all the way to the 80s and the younger one will speak of the modern times and what transpired in the latest years. This game was meant to tie in the previous one and give some context to the story.

SEAT Mii Simulator

Demo

SEAT Mii Simulator is a serious game project, it was meant to showcase some of the SEAT Mii features and simulate a project that would be presented to a non-gaming company.
